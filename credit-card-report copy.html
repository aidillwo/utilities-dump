Smart-use tips

Small habits that meaningfully raise her real return.

28

Time big Shopee buys

Spend on the 28th (Pay Day) or campaign days (9.9, 11.11, 12.12) to unlock the 5x coin tier instead of the 2x normal rate.

0%

Check EPP partners first

For the MacBook, confirm the store is a Maybank 0% EzyPay partner before buying, and check the tenure (6/12/24 months). Otherwise it falls back to 9% p.a.

✓

Pay in full, monthly

Settle the whole statement balance every cycle to dodge 15% p.a. interest that would cancel out all rewards.

⏱

Mind promo deadlines

0% EPP promotions are time-limited and change often. Verify the current offer at the point of purchase.
